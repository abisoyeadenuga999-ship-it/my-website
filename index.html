AB Rice Recipe

A step-by-step recipe for cooking rice using only HTML content.

Difficulty: Easy

Student ID: ALT/SOE/BAR/026/0133

Ingredients

  • 1 cup long-grain white rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil (optional)

Instructions

  1. Rinse the rice in a fine mesh sieve under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. In a pot, combine the rinsed rice, water, salt, and optional butter or oil.
  3. Bring the mixture to a boil over medium-high heat.
  4. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low and cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid.
  5. Simmer for 15–18 minutes without lifting the lid.
  6. Turn off the heat and let the rice rest, covered, for 5 minutes.
  7. Fluff the rice gently with a fork before serving.

Tips

  • Use a 1:2 rice-to-water ratio for fluffy rice.
  • If the rice is still firm after cooking, add a little more water and cook for 2–3 more minutes.
  • For extra flavor, cook the rice in broth instead of water.
